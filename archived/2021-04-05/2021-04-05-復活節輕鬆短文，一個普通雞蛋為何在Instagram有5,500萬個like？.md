<figure class="image"><img src="https://assets.matters.news/embed/026a32f4-8cad-43de-aaaa-73c032f688d0.jpeg" data-asset-id="026a32f4-8cad-43de-aaaa-73c032f688d0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><figcaption><span>在有Instagram上有5,500萬個like的雞蛋。</span></figcaption></figure><p>復活節標誌之一是復活節彩蛋，Instagram平台最著名的「彩蛋」，其實是一粒平平無奇的雞蛋，卻有5,500萬個like。</p><p>2019年，一個名為world_record_egg的帳戶發布一張雞蛋照片，希望大家合力打破美國名媛Kylie Jenner女兒照片的Like記錄（當時有1,800多萬），沒想到短短十天已有3,800多萬個like，成為Instagram最多Like數的照片，連帳戶也吸引逾500萬人追隨。之後一個月，帳戶繼續發布四張裂開的雞蛋照片，平均仍有一千萬個like，十分誇張。及後此帳戶的內容變成雞蛋的漫畫及動畫，可能熱潮已過，追隨者近來不增反減，然而首張雞蛋照片Like數至今逾5,500萬，遙遙領先第二名。網絡世界，果然無奇不有，說不定一張平凡的蘋果照片，會是下一個世界記錄。</p><p>Easter holiday reminds me of the most famous egg on Instagram with 55 million likes. An account named world_record_egg posted a photo of an egg in 2019, hoping the image can break the Like record of Kylie Jenner's daughter's image(over 18 million). Unexpectedly, the image received 38 million(55 million now) likes in just ten days and set a new record on Instagram. Even the account has attracted over 5 million followers. The following month, the account continued to post four photos of cracked eggs, and there were still 10 million likes on average. The craze may have passed after the record, and the number of followers was decreased recently.
